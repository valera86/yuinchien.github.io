Origami Study

A design study of origami to explore forms, colors and motion. Primitive vectors such as polygons and circles are programmed in JS to behave like paper, they fold freely to create structured layers and shapes. Checkout the live demo: yuinchien.com/projects/origami.
A series of visual study exploring origami compositions and colors, I hope to create imagery for personal memories and poetic imagination.
